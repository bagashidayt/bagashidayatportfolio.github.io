Recent Projects

Project 1

Research Based Learning

a final project from a basic chemistry course, which is to design a battery using the principles of redox reactions. In the design used bleach for clothes and metal coins and from this reaction a battery with a power of 2.5 amps was obtained.

Project 2

Capstone Design Project

Introduction and pre-design for titanium dioxide production process with ilmenite as raw material which consists of digestion, reduction, separation, crystallization, hydrolysis, and calcination processes including several details such as mass and energy balance, equipment design, plant layout, utilities, management, and evaluation economy.

Project 3

Internship Report

Analyzed the efficiency of the fractionation column, sewage treatment systems, water treatment systems, and Safety, Health and Environmental management systems in PPSDM Migas, research of raw materials used for the production process and the products produced, and successfully conducted an analysis of the efficiency of the fractionation column by producing an efficiency of 69.98%

Project 4

Final Project

analyze the causes of wax deposition in pipes, analyze the effect of pipe diameter on wax deposition, analyze the effect of using insulation on pipes on wax deposition, and analyze the effect of insulation thickness on wax deposition.
